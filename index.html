Creating a new review

Catalog your food reviews by creating a new review entry by clicking the "+" in the nabigation menu. Add a photo of the dish, your rating, and notes about your experience.

Accessing your new review

Once you save your review entry, you can find it in your catalog by clicking the "grid" in the navigation menu.

Delete your new review

You can delete existing reviews in your catalog by clicking on a review entry, and then clicking the "trashcan" delete icon.
